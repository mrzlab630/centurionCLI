# ORATOR — Facebook Platform Reference

> Source: WordStream 2026, SocialPilot, PostEverywhere, Metricool, SocialBee

## Algorithm Mechanics (2026)

### Ranking Process (4 steps)
1. **Inventory** — all available content from followed accounts + recommendations
2. **Signals** — engagement, relationship, content type, recency
3. **Predictions** — what will user interact with?
4. **Relevance Score** — final ranking

### Key Changes 2026
- **50% of Feed** is now recommended content from accounts you DON'T follow
- **UTIS Model** (User True Interest Survey) — Meta surveys users in-feed: "How well does this match your interests?"
- **Groups > Pages** — Group posts get 3-5x more organic reach than Page posts

### Ranking Signals
| Signal | Weight | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Shares | HIGHEST | Strongest distribution signal |
| Comments (5+ words) | Very High | Weighted 3x over short comments |
| Saves | High | "I'll come back to this" |
| Reactions (Love, Wow) | Medium | Weighted higher than simple Like |
| Likes | Low | Baseline signal |
| "Meaningful interactions" | Multiplier | Friends/family commenting = extra weight |

## Content Strategy

### What Works
1. **Groups** — 1.8B monthly active Group users, organic reach goldmine
2. **Native video** — especially Live video (6x more interactions than regular)
3. **Reels** — highest reach format on Facebook
4. **Photos** — highest Feed engagement
5. **Events** — built-in RSVP and reminder system
6. **Polls** — easy engagement driver in Groups

### Content Mix
| Type | Purpose | Reach |
|---|---|---|
| Reels | Growth + reach | HIGHEST |
| Photos | Engagement | HIGH |
| Live Video | Deep engagement | HIGH (6x regular) |
| Text posts | Discussion | MEDIUM |
| Links | Drive traffic | LOW (link penalty) |

### Groups Strategy (Primary Focus)
- Create or join relevant Groups (crypto, trading, Solana)
- **Be a contributor, not a promoter** (same as Reddit)
- Answer questions, share insights, be helpful
- Group posts appear in members' Feed with priority
- Admin tools: polls, featured posts, announcements

---

## Crypto on Facebook
- Less crypto-native than Twitter — more retail/mainstream audience
- **Groups are key:** Solana trading groups, DeFi communities
- Facebook Ads targeting for crypto is RESTRICTED (work within policy)
- Content must avoid: investment advice, guaranteed returns, misleading claims
- Facebook is good for EDUCATION content about crypto (mainstream audience)

---

## Timing & Frequency
- **Frequency:** 3-5 posts/week on Page, daily engagement in Groups
- **Peak hours:** Tuesday-Thursday, 9 AM - 1 PM
- **Consistency matters** — algorithm rewards regular posting
- **Video:** Upload native (not YouTube links) for maximum reach

---

## Anti-Patterns
- Posting links without context (link penalty + low engagement)
- Engagement bait ("Tag 3 friends!") — explicitly penalized since 2018
- Ignoring Groups in favor of Page-only strategy
- Auto-posting from other platforms (lower reach)
- Posting at low-activity hours
- Ignoring comments (signals inactive Page)
- Overly promotional Group posts (ban risk)
