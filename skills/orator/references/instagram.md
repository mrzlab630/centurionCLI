# ORATOR — Instagram Platform Reference

> Source: Hootsuite 2026, Buffer, Sprout Social, TrueFuture Media, Clixie AI

## Algorithm Mechanics (2026)

### Multiple Algorithms
Instagram uses SEPARATE ranking systems for: Feed, Reels, Stories, Explore, Search.

### Key Ranking Signals (by priority)
| Signal | Weight | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Shares (DM sends) | HIGHEST | "I want my friend to see this" — strongest signal 2026 |
| Saves | Very High | "I'll come back to this" |
| Watch time | High | Especially for Reels |
| Comments | High | Longer comments weighted more |
| Likes | LOW | Weakest signal in 2026 |

### Originality Score (NEW 2026)
- AI detects recycled clips, TikTok watermarks, verbatim reposts
- Reposted content = SEVERELY tanked reach
- Original content = algorithmic reward
- **Rule:** Always create original or significantly adapted content

### Content Type Reach
- **Reels:** Highest reach (35% of screen time, 2B monthly users)
- **Carousels:** Highest saves (educational content)
- **Stories:** Retention (keeps you top of mind)
- **Static posts:** Lowest reach but highest feed engagement

## Content Formats

### Reels (Primary Growth Tool)
```
[0-2s] HOOK — Text overlay + movement. Must capture in 2 seconds.
[2-15s] SETUP — Context for the value.
[15-60s] VALUE — Tutorial, reveal, story climax.
[Last 3s] CTA — "Save this for later" / "Share with someone who needs this"
```
- Optimal: 15-60 seconds (60-90s for tutorials)
- Trending audio = algorithmic boost (narrow window, act fast)
- Captions/subtitles mandatory (80% watch without sound)
- 2.46% engagement rate benchmark

### Carousel (Save Machine)
- 10 slides max
- Slide 1: Hook (bold headline)
- Slides 2-9: One point per slide, visual, digestible
- Slide 10: CTA + save prompt
- Best for: Tutorials, tips, comparisons, "X vs Y"

### Caption Formula
```
[Hook line — question or bold statement]

[2-3 paragraphs of value — break with line spacing]

[CTA — "Save this" / "Tag someone" / "Comment your X"]

[3-7 hashtags — in caption, not comments]
```
Max 2,200 characters.

---

## Hashtag Strategy
- **3-7 hashtags** (not 30 — that era is over)
- Mix: 2 broad (#crypto, #solana) + 3 niche (#solanadefi, #copytrading) + 1-2 trending
- In caption (not first comment — algorithm changed)
- Alt text on images for discovery (SEO signal)

## Optimal Content Mix (per week)
| Type | Frequency | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Reels | 3-4 | Reach + new followers |
| Carousels | 2-3 | Saves + authority |
| Stories | Daily (3-7) | Retention + engagement |
| Static posts | 1-2 | Feed presence |

## Timing & Frequency
- **1-2 posts/day** + Stories = optimal
- **Peak hours:** When YOUR followers are active (check Insights)
- **General:** Tue-Thu mid-morning and early evening
- **Consistency:** Regular posting > sporadic bursts

---

## Anti-Patterns
- TikTok watermarks on Reels (Originality Score death)
- 30 hashtags (spam signal since 2024)
- Hashtags in first comment (moved to caption)
- Ignoring Reels (missing 35% of platform reach)
- Stock-looking content (algorithm rewards authenticity)
- Not using alt text (missed SEO opportunity)
- Buying followers/engagement (detection = shadowban)
