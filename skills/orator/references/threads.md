# ORATOR — Threads (Meta) Platform Reference

> Source: MomentumHive 2026, RecurPost, QuickFrame, Metricool, MarketingAgent Blog

## Algorithm Mechanics (2026)

### Core Principle
**Conversations > Followers.** A small account creating discussion outperforms a large account posting monologues.

### Key Ranking Signals
| Signal | Weight | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Replies (meaningful) | HIGHEST | Back-and-forth discussion > passive likes |
| Reply depth | Very High | Multi-reply chains = quality conversation |
| Early engagement | High | First 30-90 minutes critical |
| Shares | High | "I want others to see this conversation" |
| Likes | Low | Weakest signal (same as Instagram) |

### "Dear Algo" Feature
Users can directly tell algorithm what they want more/less of — content quality matters more than gaming signals.

### Growth Opportunity Window
- Threads = last platform where 0 → 100K possible without suppression
- **Window closes by Q4 2026** — early movers have massive advantage
- 73.6% higher engagement rate than X (Buffer 2026 benchmarks)

## Content Strategy

### What Works on Threads
1. **Conversation starters** — Open-ended questions that invite discussion
2. **Hot takes** — Informed opinions that spark debate
3. **Personal stories** — Authenticity performs extremely well
4. **Behind-the-scenes** — Building in public resonates
5. **Quick tips** — Bite-sized actionable advice
6. **Community questions** — "What's your experience with X?"

### Tone
- **Informal** — no need for perfect grammar/punctuation
- **Conversational** — write like you talk
- **Authentic** — brands showing personality > corporate voice
- **Question-based** — end posts with questions to drive replies

### Content Format
```
[Opening hook — statement or question]

[1-2 sentences of context or opinion]

[Question to audience — drives replies]
```
Max 500 characters. Short and punchy wins.

---

## Optimal Posting
- **Frequency:** 5-7 posts/week recommended mix:
  - 2 educational posts
  - 3 engagement/discussion posts
  - 1 personal story
  - Daily engagement with others' posts
- **Timing:** First 30-90 min engagement window critical
- **Cross-posting from Instagram:** Possible but native posts perform 2x better

---

## Anti-Patterns
- "Like if you agree!" — PENALIZED explicitly
- "Follow for more!" — PENALIZED
- Long-form essays (wrong platform — keep it conversational)
- Corporate/polished tone (Threads rewards raw authenticity)
- Not replying to comments (kills algorithm momentum)
- Cross-posting identical Instagram captions (feels lazy, lower reach)
